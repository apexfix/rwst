About Us

Adelaide Renew Wood, Stone & Paint

We specialize in professional wood floor sanding and polishing, furniture refinishing, stone maintenance and sealing, as well as interior and exterior paint restoration. With years of industry experience, our mission is to help every client rediscover the original beauty of their home or business space.

Our team is committed to using eco-friendly materials and processes, ensuring minimal impact on both you and the environment, while providing outstanding results.

Our Services

Wood Floor Sanding & Polishing

Revive your worn-out hardwood floors with our professional sanding and polishing services. We repair scratches, remove old finishes, and apply protective coatings for extended durability.

Furniture Refinishing

From antique wooden chairs to modern cabinets, we offer deep cleaning, sanding, recoloring and polishing services to bring your furniture back to life.

Stone Care & Sealing

Whether you have marble, granite, or engineered stone, our polishing and sealing solutions effectively remove stains and scratches, restoring a natural shine.

Paint Restoration

We provide comprehensive paint services for interior and exterior walls, doors, and windows, using premium, eco-friendly paints for a safe and elegant finish.

FAQ

How long does it take to refinish wood floors?

It depends on the size and condition of the floor. We use high-quality, low-dust sanding equipment to minimize mess and typically complete most residential projects within a few days.

Will polishing stone surfaces cause any damage?

We employ gentle yet effective techniques to restore shine and remove scratches, protecting the longevity of your stone surfaces.

Are your paint products environmentally friendly?

Yes. We prioritize the use of eco-friendly, low-VOC paints to ensure better air quality and minimal impact on your health.
